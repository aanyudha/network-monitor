# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Heisenberg Network Monitor
community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Assuming good intent while giving clear feedback.
- Welcoming people of different experience levels.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate them.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and when an individual is
representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Please report unacceptable behavior through a private security or maintainer
channel described in `SECURITY.md`.
